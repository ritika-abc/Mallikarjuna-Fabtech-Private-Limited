Non-woven fabrics are a growing part of many industries due to their ability to be tailored for specific uses, providing a balance of performance, sustainability, and cost-effectiveness.

Non-Woven Fabrics

Material 100% Virgin Polypropylene

Non-woven fabric is a type of fabric that is manufactured by bonding fibers together, rather than weaving or knitting them like traditional fabrics. This process can involve various methods, such as heat bonding, mechanical bonding, or chemical bonding, depending on the intended use of the fabric.
